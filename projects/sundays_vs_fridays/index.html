Friday vs Sunday Hitting

Exploring how player performance shifts from Friday to Sunday — fatigue, recovery, and lineup trends.

Project Summary

Using game-level data from the 2025 season, this study measures differences in OPS between Friday and Sunday contests. The findings reveal subtle patterns in player fatigue, bullpen effectiveness, and offensive output, providing new insight into scheduling and rest strategy.
