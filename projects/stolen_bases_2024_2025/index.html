2024–2025 Stolen Base Trends

Analyzing stolen base attempts, success rates, and team strategies across two MLB seasons.

Project Summary

With rule changes increasing base sizes and limiting pickoff attempts, MLB saw a surge in stolen base activity. This analysis explores which players and teams benefited most, and whether the 2025 season sustained or reversed the 2024 momentum.
