2025 RISP + High-Leverage OPS

How MLB hitters perform when the pressure’s on.

Project Summary

This analysis compares player OPS in RISP (Runners in Scoring Position) and high-leverage situations during the 2025 season. The goal was to identify which players thrive under pressure — and which falter. The data also highlights the Yankees’ team performance in late-game scenarios.
